Jyun-Ting Song

I am a second-year master's student at the Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Kris Kitani. Previously, I earned my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan Normal University, where I was advised by Prof. Jacky Baltes.

My research interest lies in the intersection of Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Robotics. Currently, I am working on human pose estimation and human-object contact detection. I love humans, and I am interested in understanding them. I hope my reseach can contribute to this direction.
